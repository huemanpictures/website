// Our Origin Story

It Started in 5th Grade Math Class.

Two kids sat next to each other in math and realized they were both funnier than whatever was on the chalkboard. Twenty-something years later, they started a production company.

Hueman Pictures is Damien Boykin and Ryan Parr. Damien trained in acting at Stella Adler. Ryan studied filmmaking at UT Austin. Both started making films at 16. Together, they built a studio to tell the stories that only they could tell.

The name? It's a play on the word human — because every story we tell has one at the center of it.

// What We Do

FROM SCRIPT TO
SCREEN.

We're not a one-trick pony. We handle the whole production — from the first bad draft to the final export.

✍️

WRITING

Original scripts, series concepts, sketch ideas, and character bibles. We develop stories from the ground up — finding the funny and the human in every premise.

🎬

FILMING

Mockumentary. Sketch. Narrative. Live performance. We shoot it all with an eye for character — because how a scene looks should serve how it feels.

🎞️

EDITING

The cut is where comedy lives or dies. We shape pacing, rhythm, and tone in post — whether it's our original content or yours.
